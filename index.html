Context

Automotives are the design, development, manufacturing, marketing, and selling or anything pertaining to motorized vehicles. Technology is used a lot in automotives for things like safety, efficiency, comfort, performance, and production of vehicles.

The Present

Waze

Waze which is a navigation app that helps people avoid traffic, find the fastest route, it notifies you where you should be more careful, and it tells you where the cheapest gas is.

Blender

Blender is a 3D modeling program that lets designers make new designs for anything, including cars.

Auto Care

Auto Care is an app that helps people manage their car maintenance needs, helps them get service on their car and it helps lower costs for maintaining their car.

Airbags

Airbags are bags inside of the car that inflate in a few milliseconds in the event of a collision or crash in order to protect the driver and passengers of the car from serious injuries.

ABS

ABS(Anti-lock Braking Stystem) is a system that maximizes braking action at each individual wheel to prevent lock-up, so the driver can retain steering.

Backup Cameras

Backup Cameras are another piece of hardware that show the rear view from the car to help the driver avoid hitting pedestrians or other obstacles.
