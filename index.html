Waze

Waze which is a navigation app that helps people avoid traffic, find the fastest route, it notifies you where you should be more careful, and it tells you where the cheapest gas is.

Blender

Blender is a 3D modeling program that lets designers make new designs for cars.

Auto Care

Auto Care is an app that helps people manage their car maintenance needs, helps them get service on their car and it helps lower costs for maintaining their car.

Airbags

Airbags which are bags that inflate in a few milliseconds in the event of a collision or crash in order to protect the driver and passengers of the car.

ABS

ABS which is a system that maximizes braking action at each individual wheel to prevent lock-up, so the driver can retain steering.

Backup Cameras

Backup Cameras are another piece of hardware that help show the rear view from the car to help the driver avoid hitting pedestrians or other obstacles.
